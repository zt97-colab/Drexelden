Chestnut Square

$979 - $1879

Chestnut Square offers a variety of student-friendly amenities, including fully equipped apartments, high-speed internet, 24-hour fitness and recreation centers, academic support, private study rooms, and on-site dining, with convenient access to Drexel University and the University of Pennsylvania.

The Summit

$909 - $2174

The Summit at University City offers student-friendly amenities such as fully furnished apartments, high-speed internet, a 24-hour fitness center, recreation spaces with arcade games, movie theater, multimedia area, a sky lounge, and an outdoor kitchen. It also features academic support with private and group study lounges, an Academic Success Center, and includes all utilities. The location is conveniently close to Drexel University and transportation options.

University Crossings

$909 - $1769

University Crossings provides amenities such as fully furnished apartments with private and shared bedrooms, a 24-hour fitness center, recreation areas with arcade games, and an Academic Success Center. It also offers utilities included in rent, high-speed internet, a community lounge, private study spaces, and Wi-Fi throughout the property. The location is close to Drexel University, dining, retail, and public transport options.
